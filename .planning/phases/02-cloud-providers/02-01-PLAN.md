       - `mimeToFilename(norm string) string` — unexported. Switch on normalized MIME: `audio/ogg`->`audio.ogg`, `audio/mpeg`->`audio.mp3`, `audio/mp4`->`audio.mp4`, `audio/wav`/`audio/x-wav`->`audio.wav`, `audio/webm`->`audio.webm`, `audio/flac`->`audio.flac`, default->`audio.bin`.
